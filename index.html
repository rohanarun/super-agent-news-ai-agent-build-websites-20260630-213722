How an AI agent builds a website with Super

1. Research & planning

The agent browses competitors, references trends, and drafts a structure — similar to what founders do manually.

2. Generate real files

HTML, CSS, content, and assets are created as actual files — not just chat output.

3. Push & publish

Super can operate GitHub, hosting dashboards, or CMS admin panels with your approval.

4. Reuse the cache

Thanks to a computer-use cache, future edits reuse known steps instead of starting from scratch.
